Features include:

  • 4 Hardware MIDI Ouputs (both 3.5mm TRS and 5-Pin DIN connectors)
  • 1 Hardware MIDI Input
  • USB MIDI (MicroUSB)
  • Arpeggiator
  • Piano Roll Editor
  • Randomizer

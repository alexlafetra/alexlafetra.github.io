+----------------------+
|Hiding/showing objects|
+----------------------+

H - hide selected
Sh+H - hide all except for selected
Option+H - show all
Sh+click on collection - toggle visibility for everything in collection (also toggle renderness)

+------------+
|Mesh Editing|
+------------+

tab - toggle edit/object mode
Cmd + B - Bevel edges (scroll to change number of faces)
